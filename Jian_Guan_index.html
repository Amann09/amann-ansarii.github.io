    Jian Guan 关 健

    He is now a fourth-year graduate student in the Conversational AI Lab (CoAI), Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, under the supervision of Prof. Minlie Huang. Before joining CoAI, he received his bachelor’s degree in the Department of Physics at Tsinghua University. Here are his cv and 简历.

    His research interests lie in open-ended natural language generation and evaluation[must-read paper list], etc.
